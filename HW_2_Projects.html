Come Eat With Me

friends addition

Inspired by the viral sensation of the show "Come Eat with Me," I took it upon myself to initiate a similar experience within our close-knit group. With a shared love for food and dinner parties, I crafted the rules and guidelines to ensure a memorable and enjoyable dining adventure for all participants. I wanted this game to make us closer after starting to stay away from each other. Drawing from the show's premise, I encouraged each member to take turns hosting themed dinners at their homes, showcasing their culinary skills.
From Italian feasts to Asian fusion extravaganzas, each gathering became an opportunity to explore diverse cuisines and bond over shared meals. Additionally, I established a tradition of making up activity that will make us closer and will bond us.
Through this initiative, we not only indulged in delicious food but also cultivated a sense of community and belonging within our group, making "Come Eat with Me" more than just a meal, but a cherished tradition. That I hope will never happen again.
When competitiveness becomes excessive, it can breed an atmosphere of tension and discord among individuals. Instead of fostering healthy competition, an excessive competitive spirit may overshadow the value of teamwork and mutual support. It's important to strike a balance between healthy competition and cooperation, recognizing that true success often lies in collective efforts and shared achievements.
Maybe next time we will just have a lovely dinner party with good food, nice alcohol and a civilized and pleasant conversation.

FOTD

food of the date

In a playful jab at the trend of influencers' OOTD, I introduced a quirky segment into my close friends story called "FOTD" - Food of the Day. This corner evolved into a delightful showcase of my culinary adventures. With each story, I captured moments of excitement and discovery as I brought unique snacks to school and filling lunches.
As time went on, "FOTD" grew beyond mere presentation to include the essence of homemade goodness, as I started sharing recipes for the delectable treats featured. This fusion of humor, culinary creativity, and generosity became a cherished addition to our shared experiences, blending laughter with the joys of discovering and delicious homemade fare.
As the days passed, the popularity of "FOTD" grow popularity with friends eagerly anticipating the next culinary creation. It wasn't long before the segment transcended mere presentation, evolving into a celebration of homemade goodness, complete with recipes for the mouthwatering treats featured. This delightful fusion of humor, culinary ingenuity, and generosity became a cherished fixture in our shared experiences, seamlessly blending laughter with the joys of savoring and sharing delicious homemade fare. As the segment gained momentum, I found myself pleasantly surprised by the influx of recipes and culinary inspirations shared by friends, fostering a beautiful exchange of culinary creativity and camaraderie.
